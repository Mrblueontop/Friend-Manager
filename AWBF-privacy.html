Privacy Policy for Are We Best Friends

Effective date: November 7, 2025

Data Usage

Are We Best Friends analyzes publicly available Roblox data to determine how similar you and your friends are. This includes information such as your username, display name, avatar, games you’ve played or own, and other public Roblox profile details.

The extension calculates a similarity percentage based on this public data to show how close you and your Roblox friends are.

Data Storage

No personal or sensitive data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any external servers. All operations, including similarity calculations, are performed locally within your browser.

Permissions

The extension uses standard browser permissions such as activeTab, tabs, storage, and host_permissions for Roblox-related domains. These permissions are required only for displaying and comparing public Roblox data and are not used for tracking or collecting private information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. All processing is done locally and temporarily on your device.

Third-Party Services

The extension connects only to official Roblox APIs to access public data. It does not use analytics, advertisements, or tracking tools.

Children’s Privacy

This extension is safe for all Roblox users. It does not collect or store any personal data from children or any users.

Consent

By using Are We Best Friends, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. The latest version will always be available on this page with the updated effective date.
